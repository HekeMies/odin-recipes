Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, an Italian culinary masterpiece, layers wide pasta sheets with ricotta or béchamel, hearty meat sauce, and a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan. The result is a symphony of flavors and textures—the pasta maintains a perfect bite, while the savory meat and cheesy richness meld into a cohesive delight. Baked to golden perfection, each slice reveals a harmonious balance of crispy top layers and tender, flavorful depths, making lasagna a timeless and comforting classic.

Incredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodels
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
